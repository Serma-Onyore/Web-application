AN ARTIST OF THE FLOATING WORLD

An artist of the floating world is a novel that was published in 2010 by Kazuo Ishiguro. It narrates the story of a man named Masuji Ono, his family together with those around him since 1939 to 1950. moreover it illustrates the suffering of the Japanese and the damage caused to them by the second world war. Among these are death of children such as Kenji, Destruction of property such as Masuji Ono's house and change of attitude in the inhabitants of Japan such as the president of Kimura company.It also portrays different aspects of culture such as harikiri and the negotiations taken before marriage. This novel illustrated all this in details.
